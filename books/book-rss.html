Mastering Rich Site Summary for Blogs, News, and Automation

Learn RSS (Rich Site Summary) is your complete beginner-friendly guide to understanding, creating, and using RSS feeds in today’s digital world. Whether you run a blog, manage a website, publish news, share podcasts, or simply want to organize online content more efficiently, this book teaches you everything you need to know—step by step.
You will learn how RSS works, how to read feeds, how to create your own XML-based RSS files, and how to publish them online. Clear explanations, real examples, and practical walkthroughs make this book perfect for beginners, students, content creators, developers, and anyone interested in web technologies.
Inside the book, you’ll discover:

  • What RSS is and why it’s still widely used

  • How RSS readers and aggregators work

  • The structure and components of an RSS feed

  • How to build your first RSS feed manually

  • Hosting, validating, and publishing your feed

  • How to use RSS for blogs, news websites, and podcasts

  • Automation ideas using RSS feeds

  • Troubleshooting and best practices for clean, valid feeds

If you want a simple, practical, and easy-to-understand guide to RSS, this book will help you master it in no time.
